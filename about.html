About SafeWalk

Over 36 million people globally suffer from severe visual impairments â€” a number expected to double within the next 30 years. Meanwhile, more than 270,000 pedestrians die on roads annually. Navigating urban environments remains a challenge and a danger for the visually impaired.

SafeWalk was built to address this issue. We believe that mobility and safety are human rights, and advances in computer vision and language modeling can be leveraged to deliver actionable, real-time environmental insights to people who need it most.

Our target users are visually impaired individuals living in urban areas, with access to a smartphone. The application is designed to be simple to use, responsive to environmental changes, and extensible to additional platforms like smart glasses or canes in the future.
